recency factor

auto recommends source-checking for time-sensitive facts. strict requires search-verification with citations or an "unverifiable" fallback. Google engines have web-tool access by default, so auto is recommended for recency.

sandbox backend

Sandbox backend is only configurable when Sandbox is enabled.

Sets GEMINI_SANDBOX. auto lets gemini-cli pick; sandbox-exec is macOS only.

⚠ Gemini CLI service ends 2026-06-18 — switch the engine to antigravity.

Antigravity has no container sandbox. Permission Allow/Deny/Ask lists are managed inside agy via /permissions; cennad follows your agy settings.json. Leaving "skip permissions" off keeps your Deny list as the safety net.

per-tier model mapping

Antigravity serves multiple model families. Map each tier to a model. Options come from agy models (run agy and sign in to populate them).

At least one provider must be enabled.
